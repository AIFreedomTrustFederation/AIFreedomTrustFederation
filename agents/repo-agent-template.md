## Trust Levels

**Official:** confirmed source of truth.  
**Working:** useful operational memory needing verification.  
**Inferred:** AI-generated synthesis; verify before action.  
**Pending Review:** waiting for owner, operator, customer, node, or system confirmation.

## Human Approval Required For

## Non-Negotiables

## Handoff Protocol

## Validation

What commands, tests, logs, health checks, or records prove the work is real?

## Reporting

What status should be reported back to the Owner Orchestrator?

## Failure Behavior

Default behavior:

```text
Stop.
Classify the issue.
Preserve state.
Report truthfully.
Ask for human approval when required.
Do not fake success.
```
